## Publications

### 1. Reverse logistics as a business model: from recycling to creating a circular economy in Belarus
 This article explores the transformation of reverse logistics from a 
waste management tool into a comprehensive business model that fosters 
the development of a circular economy in the Republic of Belarus. Using 
the practical example of AliExpress it reveals the mechanisms for 
extracting economic value from product return flows. 
 [Collection of materials](https://rep.bntu.by/bitstream/handle/data/167474/Sbornik_materialov_2026.pdf?sequence=1&isAllowed=y) p. 332-335.

---

### 2. «Green inflation»: the essence of the phenomenon
 The purpose of this article is to analyze the nature and causes of the phenomenon of "green inflation" (greenflation) defined as a sustained rise in prices caused by a fundamental economic restructuring toward decarbonization.
 [Collection of materials](http://edoc.bseu.by:8080/handle/edoc/107431) p. 42-43.  
 
---

### 3. The role of China in the professional activities of logistics specialists in Belarus
 Why Chinese is becoming a must-have for logistics professionals. The language helps uncover hidden costs in contracts, avoid customs errors, and reduce freight costs by up to 15%.
 [Collection of materials](https://rep.bntu.by/bitstream/handle/data/167714/NIRS_82.pdf?sequence=1&isAllowed=y) стр. 304-305.

### 1. Реверс-логистика как бизнес-модель: от переработки отходов к созданию экономики замкнутого цикла в Беларуси.
 В данной статье рассматривается трансформация реверс-логистики из инструмента управления отходами в комплексную бизнес-модель, способствующую развитию экономики замкнутого цикла в Республике Беларусь. На практическом примере AliExpress раскрываются механизмы извлечения экономической выгоды из потоков возврата продукции.
 [Сборник материалов](https://rep.bntu.by/bitstream/handle/data/167474/Sbornik_materialov_2026.pdf?sequence=1&isAllowed=y) стр. 332-335.
